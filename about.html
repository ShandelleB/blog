About

My name is Shandelle Brown and I am a lover of all things wine. I am based in Dallas with my husband and our two mini longhaired dachshunds. I spend my days working as a Front End Developer and I spend my nights drinking wine, reading, spending time with my husband and friends, traveling, cuddling with my dogs, or obsessively cleaning things because Monica Geller is my spirit animal.

I am very new to the wine world. All I can say is that I enjoy drinking it and sharing it with others. I wanted a better way to keep track of the wines I tried so blogging is how I'm going to do it!

Feel free to join me in my quest of trying new wines, relishing in old favorites, and having every excuse to eat more cheese.
